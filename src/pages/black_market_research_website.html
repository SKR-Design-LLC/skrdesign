Chapter Two

Black Market Research

Indie Rock Band

This site was built for the band, Black Market Research to showcase the band's work and upcoming shows. This was my first responsive design project. I read many articles on the subject, mainly from .Net Magazine and used the methods that appealed to me and delivered the best results. I then researched rock music industry websites of all kinds to learn the visual languages of the sites. I designed two website mock ups that would best reflect the band and the client chose. I designed and built this site with HTML5, CCS3 and responsive design. I designed all visual elements of the site including the navigation. JavaScript by Andrew Clover.

Black Market Research formed in 2009, Black Market Research, an Indie/Alt band headquartered in Nashville, TN. Nick Britt - singer, songwriter, guitar. I've been the publicist for the band since it's formation.
