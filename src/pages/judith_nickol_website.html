The website is a place for the local disc golf club to post information about their annual events, biweekly tags matches and bring attention to the fantastic local courses they access.

I wanted the site to be fun and informative. I designed the website to be easy to navigate on mobile and desktop. We get a lot of sun in Colorado, and the primary color, sky blue, was representative of this.
