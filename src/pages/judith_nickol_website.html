Judith Nickol

Canadian Artist

The design came from the crispness of a gallery. Typography is always important to a design, but on a stark site it's even more important. The labels were created to look like the information card next to a work in a gallery.

A one page website for the artist to display her works, list of galleries representing her, and providing a way for interested collectors to contact her.

Launched in 2020.
