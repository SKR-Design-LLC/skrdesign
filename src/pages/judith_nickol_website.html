Judith Nickol

Canadian Artist

A one page website for the artist to display her works, list the galleries she is showing at and provide a way for interested collectors to contact her.

The design came from the crispness of a gallery showing. Fonts were an important choice for such a stark white background. The labels were created to look like the information card next to a work in a gallery.

Launched in 2020.
