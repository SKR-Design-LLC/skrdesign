Foothill Flyers

Colorado Disc Golf Club

The website is a place for the local disc golf club, Foothill Flyest, to post information about their annual events, biweekly tags matches and bring attention to the fantastic local courses they have access to.

I wanted the site to be fun and informative. I designed the site to be easy to navigate on mobile and desktop. We get a lot of sun in Colorado, and the primary color, sky blue, was representative of this.

Pro bono project. Launched in 2019.
